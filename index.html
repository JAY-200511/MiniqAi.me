Our Story

MiniQ was founded in 2022 with a vision to revolutionize digital experiences through immersive 3D technology. What started as a passion project has grown into a full-service creative studio pushing the boundaries of digital design.

We specialize in creating interactive 3D experiences that engage audiences and tell compelling brand stories. Our team combines technical expertise with artistic vision to deliver projects that stand out in today's crowded digital landscape.

From concept to execution, we work closely with our clients to ensure every project meets their strategic goals while delivering an unforgettable user experience.
